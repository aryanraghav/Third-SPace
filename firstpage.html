How it works

Silence and loud noises are usually associated with danger, thus making us alert. It is the opposite of what we need when we want to focus or relax. Background noise on the other hand is a sound that doesn’t contain spikes in volume, that is very constant and rather predictable keeping therefore our inner alarm systems calm. Moreover, due to the high amount of different frequencies contained, background sounds have great masking abilities, reducing the negative impact of sudden spikes in external noise in our environment. While listening to such background sounds, every additional noise adds to the sounds you’re already hearing and doesn’t stand out so much anymore. Therefore, additional noises are blended in and you don’t perceive them as disturbing as you normally would. This is also why music with lyrics can bring us easily out of focus, especially when having to deal with tasks that involve writing or reading. Music tends to be more rhythmic and unpredictable and if it contains lyrics, it will easily mess with your concentration and shift your focus to the lyrics instead of the task at hand. Lastly, the right amount of background noise, not too quiet and not too loud, can also help you to think in a more abstract way enhancing creative thinking.

Why us

For Studying

Our background sounds can help you to put yourself into a relaxed study mode and to help you stay concentrated for longer periods of time. This will allow you to reduce the impact of outside distractions such as phone or social media notifications and to study in a more efficient way. Third Space is also powerful in class, as it allows students to find a calm space among all the other classmates.

For Work

Staying focused at work is more and more a challenge. Frequent interruptions, mainly in form of noise, are breaking our focus, increasing our stress level and lowering our productivity. Use Third Space to drown out the overall office noise, distracting phone conversations and the general chatter from other people.

For Sleep

Being a good sleeper is the starting point to enjoy your day and to get the most out of it. The noise of the city or your wandering mind can leave you laying in bed for several hours without being able to fall asleep. Third Space can be a great way to start a familiar night time routine, putting you at ease and helping you to fall asleep more easily.

For Relaxing

Being in nature is one of the best ways to relax, relieve stress and refuel our batteries. If you don’t have the chance to be in nature in moments when you need it, you can use Third Space to bring nature to you. Background sounds of nature are especially stress relieving as we associate them with relaxation and calmness.

For Travelling

If you’re a frequent traveller you will know that finding an environment for peace and calm that lets you focus is very challenging. Being it on your daily commute, on the bus or train, or up in the air, peace and calm is a rarity in such busy environments. Third Space can be your little helper to enter a familiar workspace in order to get things done and stay productive also on the go.
